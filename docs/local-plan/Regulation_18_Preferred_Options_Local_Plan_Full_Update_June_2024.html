The plan sets a minimum requirement of 16,525 homes (661 per annum). Housing supply includes 11,023 completions and commitments, 15,473 allocated dwellings with an additional 1,900-2,500 dwellings to follow from strategic development options. Windfall allowance is 75 dwellings per year from 2024 (1,725 total from 2024-2046). The Council includes an 8% buffer in allocations for choice and competition.
